Pancake Recipe

Description

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt

Process

  1. Mix the flour, baking soda and salt in a large bowl
  2. Add eggs and milk to the mixture and add a little bit of sugar
  3. Stir and heat the oil in the pan briefly
  4. Pour and enjoy
